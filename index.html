About
I am a postdoctoral researcher jointly at the Oxford Robotics Institute and Oxford Institute for Radiation Oncology and a Lecturer at Brasenose College. My first postdoc was at the machine learning research group (MLRG) in Oxford with Mike Osborne. I am an ex-AI Researcher at NASA FDL, and also an ex-PI at the Alan Turing Institute.

I currently work with Professor Nick Hawes developing machine learning and goal-oriented planning under uncertainty for autonomous behaviour. Jointly I am also working on deep-RL methods for AI radiotherapy dose planning with Professor Katherine Vallis and Mirada Medical as part of Cancer Research UK RadNet project in Oncology.

Previously, I have worked on machine learning for healthcare projects:
   i) investigating causal biomarkers of disease in heterogeneous multi-omics data with NASA GeneLab as part of the Astronaut Health Team at The Frontier Development Lab (FDL 2021) with NASA & SETI Institute;
   ii) exploring AI supported decision-making and explainable AI methods for early stage diagnosis of colorectal cancer from images, as part of a Data Study Group with the Alan Turing Institute, UK and OdinVision. More info here.

Prior to joining the ORI, I was a PDRA in the Machine Learning Research Group with Professor Mike Osborne, also at the Department of Engineering Science, University of Oxford. I received my PhD (2017) from the University of Leeds, with Professors Anthony G. Cohn and David C. Hogg, working on machine learning for autonomous mobile robots as part of the STRANDS project. I was awarded my MSc in Mathematics and Computational Science from the University of Manchester (2010) and a BSc in Mathematics and Statistics from Lancaster University (2008).

My research interests lie in probabilistic machine learning, robotics and healthcare. I am also interested in the impact of machine learning technologies on society and the future of human work and have published research in this area.

In a previous life I was a statistical programmer for a pharma CRO, and also a banking analyst.
